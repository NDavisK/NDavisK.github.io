About Me

I come from a home where I was always encouraged to question the way Universe works. So, when most of my colleagues hopped onto the job wagon immediately after our undergrads, I could not bring myself to do that. It was nearly impossible to constrain myself to a 5-by-5 office cubicle knowing that there are so many more things to be discovered and ideas to be exchanged. So, here I am at IIT Madras, doing something that I have come to love - Reseach. I pursue my Doctorate of Philosophy in Communication and Transportation networks, at the Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Madras, India. Apart from a full-time research role, I drown myself in Coffee and Books from time to time. Currently, indulging in some much needed #quaranTIME.
